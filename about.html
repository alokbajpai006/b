History

Our institution, which is 25 years old, is one of the pioneer institution of District Jalaun in the field of Education. Memories take us to 1994 when the seeds of Morning Star sown by Mr. Bharat Bhushan Singh with the blessing of Late Shri. Swami Krishnanand Ji. with just 30 students & classes functioned from Lower Kinder Garten to 1st in our old building near Hathi Mandir.

In 2006, the untiring efforts of our founder led to the recognition of the school as an All India Secondary school & in 2009 as an All India Senior Secondary school affiliated to C.B.S.E. Delhi.

The school which was started with 30 student now has become a group and playing a vital role for education for the boys & girls both. The school that was started in 3 rooms building, now has 6 institution: (1)Morning Star Children's Academy. (1st to 12th , C.B.S.E) Karmer Road, Van Vibhag, Orai, (2) Morning Star Kids Academy, Rajendra Nagar, Orai. (3) Morning Star Girl's Hr. Sec. School (P.G. To 10th , C.B.S.E) officer colony. Sushil nagar, Orai. (4) Morning Star Little wonders (P.G. To 8th C.B.S.E) Pachokhara Road, Ait (5) B.R. Inter College (U.P. Board)Pachokhara Road, Ait (6) Swami Shri Krishnanand Mahavidyalay (B.A. , B.Sc) Pachokhara Road, Ait.

And today we feel proud to share that the number 30 has multiplied's grown to 1800 & the school is a well reknown institution upto Sr. Sec. Level.



MASSAGE FROM THE FOUNDER & CHAIRPERSON

The core purpose of education is to provide children with a learning environment which brings out the best in them. We believe that every child is endowed with a unique set of attributes and abilities. Which need to be nurtured so that they blossom. Therefore, every child deserves an education that fosters academic excellence and all-round development, and ensures their well-being. We are inspired by these tenets in everything that we do at Morning Star.

At Morning Star, we provide a learning environment that inspire and motivates children, and encourages them to explore limitless possibilities in their quest for knowledge. Since inception, our overarching goal has been to make Morning tar a happy school, where teaching is a pleasure and learning is a joy; where learning and excelling is a passion, and culture and values have a pre-eminent place.

Our caring and committed teachers are our pillars of strength. They teach our children not just with their minds but with their hearts. They make learning enjoyable and rewarding, and imbibe in children the importance of sound values. Our excellent educational programmes are complemented by state-of-the art facilities and resources, and our focus on sports and co-curricular opportunities facilities children's holistic education.

And today we feel proud to share that the number 30 has multiplied's grown to 1800 & the school is a well reknown institution upto Sr. Sec. Level.

Archana Singh

Founder & Chairperson

Are our schools well equipped to prepare our children to face the challengers that the future holds?
